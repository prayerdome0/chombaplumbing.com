Who We Are

Chomba Plumbing & Landscaping Engineers Ltd is a premier Zambian company dedicated to delivering exceptional plumbing, landscaping, and general building services. With a strong commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for both residential and commercial clients.

Our team of skilled professionals brings years of hands-on experience to every project, ensuring that we not only meet but exceed our clients' expectations. From intricate plumbing systems to breathtaking landscape designs, we approach every task with precision, creativity, and a deep sense of responsibility.
